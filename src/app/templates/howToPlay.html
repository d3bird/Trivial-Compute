How to Play Trivial Pursuit

Trivial Pursuit is a trivia game where players compete to answer questions and collect wedges in six different categories.

Objective

Be the first to collect all six category wedges and correctly answer a final question in the center of the board.

Setup

  • Each player selects a piece and places it in the center of the board.
  • Shuffle the question cards and place them nearby.
  • Decide who goes first.

Categories

  • Blue: Geography
  • Pink: Entertainment
  • Yellow: History
  • Purple: Arts & Literature
  • Green: Science & Nature
  • Orange: Sports & Leisure

Taking a Turn

  • Roll the die and move that number of spaces in any direction.
  • Answer a question based on the color space you land on.
  • If correct, you get to roll again; if not, your turn ends.
  • If you land on a wedge space and answer correctly, you earn the wedge for that category.

Winning the Game

  • Once you have all six wedges, move back to the center hub.
  • You must land on the hub by exact roll.
  • Other players select the final question category.
  • If you answer correctly, you win!
